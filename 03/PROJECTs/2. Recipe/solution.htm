Allan's Chapati Recipe

Ingredients List
All-Purpose Flour ........................... 3 cups
Warm Water ................................... 1 cup
Vegetable Oil ................................ 4 tbsp
Sea Salt ..................................... 1 tsp
Sugar (Optional) ............................. 1 tsp
Extra Flour for Dusting ...................... 1/2 cup
Instructions for Mastery
  1. Mix the flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of oil and the warm water slowly.
  3. Knead the dough for 10-15 minutes until it is soft and non-sticky.
  4. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out thin.
  6. Brush with oil, fold into a strip, and roll into a coil.
  7. Roll the coils flat again and fry on a hot pan until golden brown.
